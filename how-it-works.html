Choose Strings and Tension

We offer a full range of Polyester and Synthetic Gut strings. Different types and brands of string can generate more spin, power, control, or feel. We recommend Hyper G or Square X for more advanced players, and any of our synthetic guts for beginners.

We recommend choosing a tension between 50 and 60 pounds. Higher tensions provide more control, and lower tensions deliver more power. If you have further questions, our team is happy to assist with choosing your ideal strings or tension.

Place an Order

Visit our "Order" page to start your order. If you need a new grip, shock absorber, or grommets, indicate it as an "Add On" in the order form. Don't worry about paying now, we accept payment through Venmo or cash after we finish stringing.

Once we recieve your order details, our team will reach out to establish a pickup or dropoff location. We are located in Falcon Heights, and offer a delivery service for $2/mile.

That's It!

Our expert stringers will string your racket on any timeline. If you're in a pinch, we offer same-day stringing for only $5 extra. Our team will reach out to arrange pickup details after your racket is completed.
