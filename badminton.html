Choose Strings and Tension

We carry Yonex BG65 ti, one of the most popular badminton strings in the world. We can order different string if necessary, but turnaround times may be longer. We can also string with any string that you provide.

We recommend choosing a tension between 20 and 30 pounds. Higher tensions provide more control, and lower tensions deliver more power. We recommend around 20-24lbs for beginner/intermediate players, and 25-28lbs for advanced players. If you have further questions, our team is happy to assist with choosing your ideal strings or tension.

Place an Order

Visit our "Order" page to start your order. We have 2 locations, one in Saint Paul and one in Minneapolis, and make sure to indicate your preference while placing your order.

Once we recieve your order details, our team will reach out to establish a pickup or dropoff location. Don't worry about paying now, we accept payment through Venmo or cash after we finish stringing.

That's It!

Our expert stringers will string your racket on any timeline. If you're in a pinch, we offer same-day stringing for only $5 extra. Our team will reach out to arrange pickup details after your racket is completed.
