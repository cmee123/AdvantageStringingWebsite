FAQs

Still have lingering questions? Somebody else was probably thinking the same thing

How often should I restring my racket?

As a general rule of thumb, we recommend restringing your racket at least 4 times a year, even if the strings aren't broken. If you are a more experienced player, you should replace your strings about every month. No matter your level, strings become "dead" over time, which leads to less power on your shots and potential injuries.

How do I choose the best strings for my game?

All the items on the "Strings" page have descriptions and statistics for the characteristics of that string. Pick the one that suits your game the best or one that helps you achieve your goals (Ex: more power). We can also help guide your decision and select the best strings for your playing style.

What machine do you string with?

We string all our rackets with the Prince Neos 1000 machine. This is one of the best machines on the markets, and was used in most tennis shops until just recently.

When and how do I pay for my order?

We usually collect payment after you receive your racket(s). This ensures you get a quality product without upfront risk. We collect payment through cash, checks, and venmo. If you wish to pay with another method, contact us and we can make arrangements.
